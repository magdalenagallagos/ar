## Hardfacing Wikipedia
Hardfacing is a metalworking process where harder or tougher material is applied to a base metal.It is welded to the base material,and generally takes the form of specialized electrodes for arc welding or filler rod for oxyacetylene and TIG welding.Powder metal alloys are used in (PTA) also called Powder plasma welding.
