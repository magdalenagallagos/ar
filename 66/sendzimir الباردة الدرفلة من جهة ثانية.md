## Sendzimir process Wikipedia
Sendzimir process (named after Tadeusz Sendzimir) is used to galvanize a steel strip by using a small amount of aluminum in the zinc bath and producing a coating with essentially no iron zinc alloy.The process guarantees high resistance and durability characteristics.About 75% of hydrogen was needed in the original.
