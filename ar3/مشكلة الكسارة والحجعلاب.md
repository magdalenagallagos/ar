## Purple urine bag syndrome Wikipedia
Purple urine bag syndrome, or PUBS is a medical syndrome where purple discoloration of urine occurs in people with urinary catheters and co existent urinary tract infection. Bacteria in the urine produce the enzyme indoxyl phosphatase. This converts indoxyl sulfate in the urine into the red and blue colored compounds indirubin and indigo.
