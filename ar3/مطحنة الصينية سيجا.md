## King Saud Medical Complex Wikipedia
King Saud Medical City is a hospital in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia is a tertiary care center for medicine and surgery especially spinal and neurosurgery is international recognized center for training in medicine and surgery. It has 1500 beds of which 180 beds are in the intensive care unit beds. By virtue of the bed capacity and patient load, KSMC is by far the largest hospital of Saudi Arabia.
