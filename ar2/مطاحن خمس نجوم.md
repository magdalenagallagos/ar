## Five Star Group
Five Stars Flour Mills has a production capacity of 1,750 metric tons daily and is the largest single flour mill by capacity in the whole country. This is in addition to a storage capacity of 150,000 metric tons of grain silos, and a discharge berth loed at Adabiya Port in Suez equipped with a 600 tons per hour grain unloader tower.
