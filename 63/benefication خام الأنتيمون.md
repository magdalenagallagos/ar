## Beneficiation Wikipedia
In the mining industry or extractive metallurgy,beneficiation is any process that improves (benefits) the economic value of the ore by removing the gangue minerals,which results in a higher grade product (concentrate) and a waste stream (tailings).Examples of beneficiation processes include froth flotation and gravity.
