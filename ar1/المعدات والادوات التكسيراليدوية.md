## Extensional and intensional definitions Wikipedia
In logic and mathematics, an intensional definition gives the meaning of a term by specifying necessary and sufficient conditions for when the term should be used. In the case of nouns, this is equivalent to specifying the properties that an object needs to have in order to be counted as a referent of the term.. For example, an intensional definition of the word "bachelor" is "unmarried man".
